What is VRET?

Virtual reality exposure therapy (VRET), also known as and computerized cognitive behavioral therapy (CCBT), is the use of virtual reality technology for psychological or occupational therapy.Patients receiving virtual reality therapy navigate through digitally created environments and complete specially designed tasks often tailored to treat a specific ailment. It is widely used as an alternative form of exposure therapy (VIVO) and imagination therapy.

Why VRET?

VRET is considered by far the safest phobia treatment method out there. The VR experience feels real although technically it isnâ€™t, which minimizes physical risk and risk of further traumatization. It is also more affordable and more feasible compared to VIVO exposure therapy, and in terms of realism, it is more realistic than imagination therapy
